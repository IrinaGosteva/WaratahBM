Welcome to Waratah Building Management
Waratah is a dedicated Building and Facilities Management specialist, servicing the commercial, residential and mixed-use properties.
Our personal approach is noticeably different to the processes of larger, corporate Building Management suppliers. To deliver the highest standards of service our ‘hands on’ team looks after your property as if it were their own. While our methods are traditional, our processes are far from old-fashioned. Systems are in place to surpass industry standards of expertise, accountability and management transparency that ensure client
confidence in our ability to enhance the value of your property. Managing Director George Carpenter has close to a decade of experience in delivering quality building management to strata properties of all sizes, from new constructions to those over 30 years old.
Based in Rhodes, Waratah is perfectly positioned to manage the needs of property developing area of owners in this constantly developing area of Sydney.
